01
Signing In
Access the member portal at abbeyton-lodge.netlify.app
1
Open the app in your browser
Visit abbeyton-lodge.netlify.app on your phone. Bookmark it or add to your home screen for easy access.
2
Enter your email and password
Type your email and password, then tap SIGN IN. If you don't have an account, tap Request access at the bottom.
3
You're in
You'll land on the main app with the bottom navigation bar. Your role shows as MEMBER.

03
Booking a Reservation
Three-step process. You can go back at any step before confirming.
1When & Where
2Who's Coming
3Confirm
1
Step 1 — When & Where
Fill in:
• Date — tap to open the date picker
• Meal — Lunch or Dinner
• Time — select from the time picker
• Room (optional) — your dining room preference (Breakfast Nook, Card Room, Pool, etc.)
• Notes (optional) — any special requests (e.g. "no chocolate", "somewhere shady")

Tap Next: Who's Coming →
2
Step 2 — Who's Coming
Your saved household members appear automatically. Tap × to remove anyone not attending.

To add a guest (someone not in your household): type their name in the Guest name... field and tap Add. Guests are labeled GUEST, members are labeled MEMBER.

Party size shows as (N/10) — max 10 per reservation.

Tap Review → when done, or ← Back to go back.
3
Step 3 — Confirm
Review the summary: Date, Time, Meal, Room, Party names, Notes.

Tap Confirm Reservation ✓ to submit. The reservation is created in Confirmed status — staff will have it on their Admin page immediately.

Tap ← Back to make changes.
ℹ️
You can only order food after the reservation is confirmed and you've been assigned to a table by staff. Check My Trips to see your reservation status.
04
My Trips
See all your upcoming and past reservations.
1
Switch between Upcoming and Past
Tap Upcoming or Past at the top to toggle. Each reservation card shows the date, time, meal type, notes, and status.
2
Tap a reservation to see details
Tap CONFIRMED → on a card to open the reservation detail page. You can view attendees, check your order status, and access messages from here.
⚠️
A reservation showing No order placed means no one in your party has ordered yet. Use the Order tab to place orders before you arrive.
05
Ordering Food
Place individual orders for each person in your party. Each person gets their own order.
1
Tap Order in the bottom nav
A list of your reservations appears. Each shows the date, time, and meal type. Green dots indicate upcoming reservations. Tap TAP TO ORDER → on the reservation you want.
2
Select a person
Attendee name pills appear at the top (e.g. Nat, Molly, Parker, Jaime, Nolan). Tap a name to switch to their order. A number badge on a name shows how many items they have in their order.
3
Browse the menu
Filter by category: All Starters Salads & Bowls Add-on etc. Each item shows a description and price.
4
Add items to the order
Tap Add next to any item. It appears in the CURRENT ORDER box at the top with a running total. If the item is already in the order, the button shows +1 and a badge shows "N in order". Use the − button to remove.
5
Repeat for each person
Tap another name pill and place their order. Orders are per-person. You can order different things for each attendee.
🔒
Orders cannot be changed after staff fires them. If an order is fired, the screen shows "This order has been fired and can no longer be edited." Menu items will have grayed-out Add buttons.
💡
You can order before arriving. Staff can also place or adjust orders for you at the table in Floor View. If you need changes after firing, message the staff.
06
Household Members
Save your family members once — they auto-populate when you book a reservation.
1
View your household
Tap Members in the bottom nav. Your household is listed: each card shows the person's name, relation (Primary, Spouse, Child), and dietary restriction tags.
2
Add a household member
Tap + Add Household Member. Fill in:
• Full Name (required)
• Relation (optional — e.g. Child, Spouse)
• Dietary Restrictions — tap to select any that apply (Dairy Free, Egg Free, Fish Allergy, Gluten Free, Halal, Kosher, Nut Allergy, Peanut Allergy, Sesame Allergy, Shellfish Allergy, Soy Free, Vegan, Vegetarian)

Tap Add Member to save.
3
Edit a member
Tap Edit on any member card. Change name, relation, or dietary restrictions. Tap Save Changes. A "Member updated" toast confirms the save.
4
Remove a member
Tap Remove on the member card. The member is removed from your household and will no longer auto-populate in future reservations.
ℹ️
Dietary restrictions saved here are automatically visible to staff when they view attendees in Floor View and print kitchen chits. Always keep these up to date.
07
Messaging Staff
Send notes to the staff about a specific reservation. Staff can reply from the Admin or Overlord panel.
1
Tap Messages in the bottom nav
You'll see a list of your reservations — past and upcoming. Each shows the date, time, and meal type. Tap TAP TO VIEW MESSAGES → on the reservation you want to message about.
2
Read the message thread
All messages for this reservation appear in order. Your messages appear left-aligned, staff replies appear on the right. Each message shows the sender's email.
3
Send a message
Type your message in the Message to staff... field and tap Send. Your message appears immediately. Staff will see it in the Admin or Overlord reservation detail with an "unread" indicator.
💡
Messages are reservation-specific. To message about your 11 AM reservation, select that reservation's thread — not the 1 PM one. Each reservation has its own separate conversation.
